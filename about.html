About Keryn

Keryn mainly writes YA fiction. She has been writing / scribbling / journaling most of her life, but began to write more intentionally in 2016. She lives in the beautiful Hawkes Bay with her husband, children, dog and part time cat (his choice). When she isn't writing she works as a GP.
